Plan Trips

Common and underserved routes will have the most conversation and the most trips per week, but any route in the system will be discoverable with browsing or with search. Complex trips with multiple stops are supported, as are turn-by-turn directions and travel time estimates.

Keep Score

Users are awarded points for giving lifts or using alternative transportation options. The platform tallies up points by person, by organization, and across entire cities. Badges or other incentives can be granted upon achieving significant milestones by the software or by organization leaders.
